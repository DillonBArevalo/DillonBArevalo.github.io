Swing Dancing!

Swing dancing is my favorite hobby!

Styles of Swing Dance

  • Lindy Hop (My favorite type)
  • Charleston (A great way to take a break from faster dances or a wonderful dance on its own!) (not updated)
  • East Coast Swing (The easy to learn variant of Swing) (not updated)
  • Balboa (Great for fast music and close connection!) (not updated)
  • West Coast Swing (To be honest, I have gripes about whether this is actually swing, but it's still a good dance) (not updated)

I think this is a pretty good place to learn about Swing Dance. It has a lot of information on it with links to more and, as far as I can tell with a quick skim, looks pretty accurate! I think it should have more information on social dancing as I think of that as the main draw and biggest part of swing dancing (not just in a personal sense, but when looking at the Swing Dance culture as a whole). I also think that videos should be more prominently linked for it to be as engaging and educational as possible.
