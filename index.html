Swing Dancing!

Swing dancing is my favorite hobby!

Styles of Swing Dance

  • [Lindy Hop](lindyhop.html) (My favorite type)
  • [Charleston](charleston.html) (A great way to take a break from faster dances or a wonderful dance on its own!)
  • [East Coast Swing](east_coast.html) (The easy to learn variant of Swing)
  • [Balboa](balboa.html) (Great for fast music and close connection!)
  • [West Coast Swing](west_coast.html) (To be honest, I have gripes about whether this is actually swing, but it's still a good dance)

Click the styles above to learn more about them!

There are many other styles not included on this page. For more information on what is included here as well as those other styles, visit the Swing Dance [Wikipedia Page](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swing_(dance)).
